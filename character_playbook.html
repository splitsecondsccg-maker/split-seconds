Character Playbook

Strategies, Key Cards, and Turn Patterns

Rogue

Rogue plays tempo attrition: poke with cheap attacks, disrupt hand and stamina timing, then execute when the opponent has limited options. It pressures decision quality first, then cashes out with darkness finish windows.

Key cards: Shakedown, Execution Window, Kidney Strike, Night Blade.

Example turn A: Slot 1 Shakedown, Slot 2 Kidney Strike, Slot 4 Execution Window.

Example turn B: Slot 1 Quick Jab, Slot 2 Flurry + Coat with Sedative, Slot 5 Night Blade.

Mauja

Mauja wins through heavy trades and punish grabs, using poison as scaling pressure over repeated rounds. The plan is to stabilize with brute frame efficiency, then convert poison depth into oversized grab damage.

Key cards: Toxic Slam, Venom Crush, Toxic Sting, Earthshatter.

Example turn A: Slot 1 Toxic Sting, Slots 2-3 Toxic Slam, Slots 4-5 Venom Crush.

Example turn B: Slots 1-2 Cleave, Slot 3 Warcry, Slots 4-5 Venom Crush.

Paladin

Paladin is defense-to-burst control: absorb pressure efficiently, build armor windows, then convert that safety into holy finish turns. It rewards stable planning and disciplined timing.

Key cards: Sanctuary, Armor Judgment, Shield Discipline, Holy Smite.

Example turn A: Slot 1 Shield Discipline, Slot 2 Sanctuary, Slots 4-5 Armor Judgment.

Example turn B: Slot 1 Medic Light, Slots 2-3 Pummel, Slots 4-5 Holy Smite.

Vampiress

Vampiress runs a bleed and lifesteal loop: keep up chip pressure while forcing awkward defense responses with grabs. The deck becomes lethal when bleed stacks are converted into direct payoff damage.

Key cards: Bloodletter Strike, Exsanguinate, Crimson Grapple, Lethal Embrace.

Example turn A: Slot 1 Bloodletter Strike, Slot 2 Crimson Grapple, Slots 4-5 Exsanguinate.

Example turn B: Slot 1 Siphon Strike, Slot 2 Vampiric Bite, Slots 3-4 Lethal Embrace.

Necromancer

Necromancer is a status engine: force bad responses through poison and control, then convert setup into high-impact spell or grab bursts. It thrives on sequencing and delayed payoffs.

Key cards: Grave Whisper, Mind Sever, Plague Grip, Spiders Swarm.

Example turn A: Slot 1 Grave Whisper, Slots 2-3 Mind Sever, Slot 5 Skull Blast.

Example turn B: Slot 1 Scare, Slot 2 Plague Grip, Slots 3-4 Spiders Swarm.

Palea

Palea is hypnotic timing control: mark with HYPNOTIZED, deny pivotal enemy moments, then convert with precise consume burst turns. The identity is trickery plus exact timing punish.

Key cards: Don't, Blink, Silken Thought, Grand Refrain.

Example turn A: Slot 1 Fae Whisper, Slot 2 Don't, Slots 3-4 Grand Refrain.

Example turn B: Slot 1 Silken Thought, Slot 2 Mesmer Snare, Slot 3 Snap Fingers, Slot 5 Fae Needle.

Ice Assassin

Ice Assassin uses fast freeze setup to enable efficient threshold burst. The strategy is to stay tempo-positive while preparing freeze states that turn ordinary attacks into lethal trades.

Key cards: Frigid Mark, Shatter Step, Break the Ice, Ice Slash (ability).

Example turn A: Slot 1 Frigid Mark, Slot 2 Ice Slash, Slot 4 Shatter Step.

Example turn B: Slot 1 Cold Wind, Slot 2 Frostbind, Slot 3 Shatter Step, Slot 5 Break the Ice.

Ice Brute

Ice Brute is a freeze bruiser that punishes aggression and cashes out on threshold grabs. It uses sturdy brute lines to survive while freeze totals climb into payoff range.

Key cards: Glacial Pummel, Shatter Grab, Frost Club (ability), Frost Hug (ability).

Example turn A: Slots 1-3 Frost Club, Slots 4-5 Shatter Grab.

Example turn B: Slot 1 Cold Wind, Slots 2-3 Glacial Pummel, Slots 4-5 Frost Hug.

Fae Brute

Fae Brute blends brute punishment with hypnotic disruption, forcing mis-timed enemy lines before finishing with consume burst attacks. It is the pressure-control hybrid of the roster.

Key cards: Trance Hook, Dreambreaker, Mesmer Snare, Puppet Strings.

Example turn A: Slot 1 Trance Hook, Slots 2-3 Dreambreaker, Slot 5 Suplex.

Example turn B: Slot 1 Mesmer Snare, Slots 2-3 Cleave, Slot 4 False Command, setup Dreambreaker follow-up.

Ice Djinn

Ice Djinn is rhythm control: negate key enemy actions, stack freeze over safe windows, then finish through deep-freeze conversion. It rewards patient sequencing and denial timing.

Key cards: Spirit Form, Frost Meditation, Absolute Zero, Ice Wall.

Example turn A: Slot 1 Frost Meditation, Slots 2-4 Spirit Form, Slot 5 Ice Spear.

Example turn B: Slot 1 Cold Wind, Slots 2-4 Absolute Zero, Slot 5 Break the Ice.

Negate-value design note: Negate cards now provide extra tempo value. Don't draws 1 on successful negate, Spirit Form applies FREEZE 1 per negated action, and Blink or Mirror Prank already negate while also dealing damage and drawing.
