# Character Strategy Playbook

This playbook summarizes each character's gameplan after the new enabler/payoff card pass.

## Rogue
Rogue plays tempo attrition: poke with cheap attacks, disrupt hand/stamina timing, then execute when the opponent's options are thin. `Shakedown` and `Kidney Strike` create pressure by shrinking responses, while `Execution Window` and `Night Blade` close when hand quality collapses.
- Key cards: `rogue_shakedown`, `rogue_execution_window`, `rogue_kidney_strike`, `darkness_night_blade`.
- Example turn A: Slot 1 `Shakedown`, slot 2 `Kidney Strike`, slot 4 `Execution Window` -> disrupt first, then cash out.
- Example turn B: Slot 1 `Quick Jab`, slot 2 `Coat with Sedative` on `Flurry`, slot 5 `Night Blade` -> keep pressure and punish low-hand states.

## Mauja
Mauja wins by trading early, then converting poison counts into oversized brute damage. `Toxic Slam` and `Toxic Sting` establish poison depth, and `Venom Crush` converts that setup into a high-leverage grab payoff.
- Key cards: `brute_toxic_slam`, `brute_venom_crush`, `poison_toxic_sting`, `brute_earthshatter`.
- Example turn A: Slot 1 `Toxic Sting`, slots 2-3 `Toxic Slam`, slots 4-5 `Venom Crush` -> stack poison then convert.
- Example turn B: Slots 1-2 `Cleave`, slot 3 `Warcry`, slots 4-5 `Venom Crush` -> force defense then punish hard.

## Paladin
Paladin plays durable control: survive efficiently, build armor windows, and convert them into holy burst turns. `Sanctuary` plus defensive lines creates armor value, and `Armor Judgment` is the dedicated armor payoff finisher.
- Key cards: `paladin_sanctuary`, `paladin_armor_judgment`, `warrior_shield_discipline`, `paladin_holy_smite`.
- Example turn A: Slot 1 `Shield Discipline`, slot 2 `Sanctuary`, slots 4-5 `Armor Judgment` -> stabilize, then strike from armor tempo.
- Example turn B: Slot 1 `Medic Light`, slots 2-3 `Pummel`, slots 4-5 `Holy Smite` -> sustain into reliable two-attack pressure.

## Vampiress
Vampiress is a bleed/lifesteal loop deck: keep chip pressure while setting bleed for grab cash-outs. `Bloodletter Strike` accelerates bleed stacks and `Exsanguinate` turns those stacks into immediate extra damage.
- Key cards: `vamp_bloodletter_strike`, `vamp_exsanguinate`, `vamp_crimson_grapple`, `vamp_lethal_embrace`.
- Example turn A: Slot 1 `Bloodletter Strike`, slot 2 `Crimson Grapple`, slots 4-5 `Exsanguinate` -> build bleed then detonate.
- Example turn B: Slot 1 `Siphon Strike`, slot 2 `Vampiric Bite`, slots 3-4 `Lethal Embrace` -> race with sustain and grab pressure.

## Necromancer
Necromancer is a status engine control deck: apply poison/hypnotic pressure, force awkward responses, then convert with burst attacks. `Grave Whisper` is the hypnotic enabler, and `Mind Sever` is the dedicated consume payoff.
- Key cards: `necro_grave_whisper`, `necro_mind_sever`, `necro_plague_grip`, `spiders_swarm`.
- Example turn A: Slot 1 `Grave Whisper`, slots 2-3 `Mind Sever`, slot 5 `Skull Blast` -> mark then burst.
- Example turn B: Slot 1 `Scare`, slot 2 `Plague Grip`, slots 3-4 `Spiders Swarm` -> disrupt line quality while poison grows.

## Palea
Palea is hypnotic timing control: apply HYPNOTIZED, negate pivotal enemy moments, then convert with technical burst cards. `Silken Thought` smooths setup consistency and `Grand Refrain` gives a true high-end consume payoff.
- Key cards: `palea_dont`, `palea_blink`, `palea_silken_thought`, `palea_grand_refrain`.
- Example turn A: Slot 1 `Fae Whisper`, slot 2 `Don't`, slots 3-4 `Grand Refrain` -> deny then punish.
- Example turn B: Slot 1 `Silken Thought`, slot 2 `Mesmer Snare`, slot 3 `Snap Fingers`, slot 5 `Fae Needle` -> refill, mark, and convert.

## Ice Assassin
Ice Assassin plays tempo conversion: build freeze quickly and attack through high-value windows when thresholds are met. `Frigid Mark` improves freeze reliability and `Shatter Step` gives efficient threshold burst.
- Key cards: `ice_assassin_frigid_mark`, `ice_assassin_shatter_step`, `ice_break_the_ice`, `ability_ice_assassin_1`.
- Example turn A: Slot 1 `Frigid Mark`, slot 2 `Ice Slash` (ability), slot 4 `Shatter Step` -> stack freeze then convert.
- Example turn B: Slot 1 `Cold Wind`, slot 2 `Frostbind`, slot 3 `Shatter Step`, slot 5 `Break the Ice` -> chain freeze pressure into cash-out.

## Ice Brute
Ice Brute is a bruiser that turns enemy aggression into freeze and then cashes out with threshold grabs. `Glacial Pummel` deepens freeze setup and `Shatter Grab` provides a second freeze-threshold payoff beside `Frost Hug`.
- Key cards: `ice_brute_glacial_pummel`, `ice_brute_shatter_grab`, `ability_ice_brute_1`, `ability_ice_brute_2`.
- Example turn A: Slots 1-3 `Frost Club` (ability), slots 4-5 `Shatter Grab` -> stack then punish.
- Example turn B: Slot 1 `Cold Wind`, slots 2-3 `Glacial Pummel`, slots 4-5 `Frost Hug` -> accelerate freeze for high burst.

## Fae Brute
Fae Brute mixes brute frame pressure with hypnotic disruption, forcing the enemy into bad timing and punishing with consume hits. `Trance Hook` provides reliable hypnotic setup and `Dreambreaker` gives brute-grade consume payoff.
- Key cards: `fae_brute_trance_hook`, `fae_brute_dreambreaker`, `mesmer_snare`, `palea_puppet_strings`.
- Example turn A: Slot 1 `Trance Hook`, slots 2-3 `Dreambreaker`, slot 5 `Suplex` -> mark then force high-threat responses.
- Example turn B: Slot 1 `Mesmer Snare`, slots 2-3 `Cleave`, slot 4 `False Command`, slots 4-5 `Dreambreaker` next cycle -> keep hypnotic pressure active.

## Ice Djinn
Ice Djinn is control cadence: deny enemy actions, stack freeze over time, then close with deep-freeze finishers. `Frost Meditation` improves resource flow and `Absolute Zero` is the dedicated freeze-depth finisher.
- Key cards: `spirit_spirit_form`, `ice_djinn_frost_meditation`, `ice_djinn_absolute_zero`, `ice_ice_wall`.
- Example turn A: Slot 1 `Frost Meditation`, slots 2-4 `Spirit Form`, slot 5 `Ice Spear` -> deny, stack freeze, and hold initiative.
- Example turn B: Slot 1 `Cold Wind`, slots 2-4 `Absolute Zero`, slot 5 `Break the Ice` -> high freeze line into explosive conversion.

## Negate Value Note
Negate cards now provide extra tempo value:
- `Don't` now draws 1 card on successful negate.
- `Spirit Form` now applies `FREEZE 1` each time it negates an enemy attack/grab.
- `Blink` and `Mirror Prank` already negate plus deal damage and draw.
